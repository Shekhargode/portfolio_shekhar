Airline Passenegr Satisfaction

  • "Airline Passenger Satisfaction" is an intern project organized by Henotic Technologies Pvt Ltd, Hyderabad, focusing and enhancing experience through AIML techniques.
  • The project utilizes a range of machine learning methods including logistic regression, decision trees, random forest classifier, SVM, KNN, and LGBM classifier to analyze passenger satisfaction data.
  • Input data encompasses 25 features such as passenger name, age, address, arrival/departure times, and ratings on airline amenities like WiFi, food quality, and seat comfort.
  • Through rigorous training, the models are fine-tuned to identify patterns and correlations within the dataset, enabling accurate prediction of passenger satisfaction levels.
  • After initial training, the top three performing models are selected, and further optimization is conducted through hyperparameter tuning to enhance their predictive capabilities.
  • The model with the highest accuracy is then employed to predict passenger satisfaction levels across the dataset.
  • The analysis reveals that only 43% of passengers are satisfied with the airline services.
